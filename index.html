Description

My favorite sport is soccer. Essentialy, it's just a game where there are boundaries around two big nets, two teams, and a ball. Both teams have to try and get the ball in the other team's net.

Rules

Rule Number 1: "You can't inflict injuries on opposing players." This will result in a yellow card, which is the warning in a game, and the red card means you get kicked out of the game.
Rule Number 2: You can't try and score the ball from inside the goalie box.
Rule Number 3:If you go out of boundaries, then the ball is given to te other team in two ways; by a throw in, or a goalie kick, depending on where the ball went out.
-These are the most essential rules to start playing soccer.
